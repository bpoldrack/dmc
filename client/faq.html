How do you protect the privacy of my data?

We don't; you do!

Any data you enter into the survey form will be made public. If you are not comfortable with sharing your data, do not enter it.

That being said, we do not ask you for any information that identifies you. You don't need an email or account to use this site, nor do we want any of that information from you. We don't use cookies or other means to track visitors. Using this website involves no 3rd-party online services, no Google Analytics, no ads, nothing (take a look at the code, and see for yourself).

We assign two random identifiers to a new submission. One for you to update your submission, and another one to access statistics computed based upon the submission. You should keep the first one to yourself, and share the latter one with anyone and everyone you desire.

Remember, we do not track people. If you lose your access URL, we have no way of helping you recover it. Keep it safe!

You said all data is public. Where is it?
Data is kept in a Git repository on Github. It is pushed there in batches every few days.
